About Me

Actor. Creator.
Chaos energy.

I'm Himikaa Bose — trained at Bristol Old Vic Theatre School, and equally at home in a dramatic scene as I am losing my mind at a Grand Prix. I've been acting since I was a kid doing short films, and I haven't stopped since.

From Bollywood to Malayalam cinema to Bengali shows to Amazon OTT — I've been around the block. Off screen, I'm a lifestyle and F1 content creator sharing everything from race weekends and motorsport deep dives to everyday life, fashion, and the chaos of living between Delhi and Bombay. Add 80+ TV commercials and 15 years of dance training and you've got the full picture.
